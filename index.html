Welcome to the Object-ive Society, a speculative web platform that explores the intricate relationship between objects, memory, and consciousness through the lens of loss. This website serves as a digital archive for objects lost by classmates, reimagining them as "Future Relics" – remnants of personal histories stripped of their immediate materiality and presented as enduring artifacts.
ABOUT

In Objective Society, the object becomes a vessel—a memory container. Our subjective experiences embed themselves into the physical world, giving objects not only sentimental but structural memory. In the absence of people, ideas, and even language, objects remain. They carry memory forward. This project asks: can we build a better infrastructure for memory? A new kind of museum that honors the lost without imposing the gaze of the archiver?

By presenting these items without personal narrative or interpretation, the project creates a space where memory lives outside the subjective. A neutral, digital terrain for relics to simply be—layered, textured, temporally dislocated, and yet preserved.
